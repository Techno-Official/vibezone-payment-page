About Vibzeo

Vibzeo is a one-to-one audio calling app developed and maintained by Techzniczone, a registered software company based in Bangalore, India. Our platform allows users to connect, talk, and earn through high-quality private audio conversations.

Our Mission

At Vibzeo, our goal is to provide a secure and engaging environment where people can enjoy private conversations while also earning rewards. Users can purchase Vibzeo Coins to access premium features, and hosts can earn from the coins they receive during calls.
