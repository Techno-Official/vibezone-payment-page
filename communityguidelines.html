Community Guidelines

Last updated: October 6, 2025

Welcome to Vibzeo — a platform built by Techzniczone, Bangalore, where users can enjoy one-to-one audio interactions in a safe, respectful, and enjoyable environment.

These Community Guidelines are designed to ensure that all users of Vibzeo can connect, communicate, and earn in a healthy and positive space. Every user, both callers and hosts, must follow these rules while using the app.

1. Respectful Communication

Be kind and respectful at all times. Harassment, abuse, threats, hate speech, or offensive behavior are strictly prohibited. Everyone deserves a safe and comfortable experience.

  • Do not use vulgar, obscene, or sexually explicit language.
  • Do not insult, bully, or demean others based on gender, race, religion, or background.
  • Do not engage in arguments or personal attacks during calls.

2. No Inappropriate or Illegal Content

Any activity or content that is illegal or inappropriate will result in immediate suspension or permanent ban.

  • No sexually suggestive, pornographic, or adult content.
  • No sharing of illegal material or requests for such activity.
  • No promoting or discussing violence, drugs, weapons, or self-harm.

3. Privacy and Consent

Respect other users’ privacy and boundaries.

  • Do not record or share conversations without explicit consent.
  • Do not share others’ personal details (phone number, address, social media, etc.) without permission.
  • Report any suspicious or unsafe behavior immediately to our support team.

4. Fair Use of the Platform

Use Vibzeo fairly and honestly.

  • Do not manipulate or cheat the coin system or earnings.
  • Do not use fake profiles, bots, or multiple accounts for exploitation.
  • Do not misuse payment features or attempt fraudulent transactions.

5. Hosts’ Responsibilities

Hosts represent the Vibzeo community and are expected to maintain professionalism and respect in all interactions.

  • Be polite, welcoming, and responsive to callers.
  • Do not use Vibzeo for dating, solicitation, or adult entertainment purposes.
  • Maintain a clean, noise-free, and distraction-free environment during calls.

6. Reporting Violations

If you encounter a user who violates these guidelines, please report them through the app or contact our support team. We review all reports carefully and take appropriate action, which may include warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans.

7. Enforcement

Techzniczone reserves the right to monitor activity for safety and moderation purposes. Repeated or severe violations of these rules may result in loss of account, earnings, or access to the app.

8. Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these guidelines from time to time to maintain a safe and respectful community. The latest version will always be available on this page.
